Frequently Asked Questions

Why are signals not updating?

Signals refresh every 60 seconds using live market data. If updates have stopped, check your internet connection and verify your API key is still valid in Settings.

How do I change my trading style?

Go to Settings and select your preferred style under Trading Style. Conservative uses stricter thresholds, Aggressive uses looser ones.

Why am I not receiving background alerts?

iOS controls background refresh timing. For best results, keep CandleSense in your recently used apps. You can also check that Background App Refresh is enabled for CandleSense in your iPhone Settings.

What does the confidence score mean?

The confidence score reflects how many technical indicators agree on the current signal direction. A higher score means stronger agreement across indicators.

How do I reset my API key?

Go to Settings, scroll to the API Key section, and tap Reset. You will be taken back to the setup screen to enter a new key.
