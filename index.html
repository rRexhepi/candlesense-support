Frequently Asked Questions

What is CandleSense?

CandleSense is a technical analysis tool that generates automated signals based on crypto market data. It uses algorithms such as RSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands, and ADX to identify potential market conditions.

Where does the market data come from?

Market data is sourced from CoinGecko. You will need a free CoinGecko Demo API key to use the app. You can get one at coingecko.com under the free Demo plan.

What do the signal levels mean?

Signals range from Strong Buy to Strong Sell. They reflect what the algorithm has detected across multiple technical indicators at your chosen profile sensitivity. They are algorithm outputs only, not recommendations.

What is the trading profile?

The trading profile (Conservative, Standard, or Aggressive) sets the sensitivity thresholds for signal generation. Conservative requires stronger confirmation before a signal fires. Aggressive fires at lower thresholds and suits active day traders who monitor positions closely.

What is guided mode?

Guided mode displays signals in plain language. It is intended for users who are less familiar with technical analysis terminology. The app is locked to Conservative profile in guided mode for safer defaults.

My API key is not working. What should I do?

Make sure you are using a CoinGecko Demo API key (not a paid plan key). You can update your key at any time in the Settings tab. If the issue persists, contact us below.
